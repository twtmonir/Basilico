welcome to basilico china restaurant

Bringing the taste
of China to you

At Basilico, we bring parties full of culinary emotions with famous set menus of Chinese cuisine.These are juicy succulent Tieu Long Bao dumplings, crispy Hong Kong roast duck with soft skin.

We serve authentic Beijing cuisine, inheriting the cooking techniques the Qing Dynasty's court cuisine. We present unique local Beijing dishes, including Zha Jiang Noodles, Aiwowo, and many more, aided by a wide variety of meticulously prepared dishes and snacks.

our menu

Chinese food culture

Basilico was born of an enthusiastic exploration of Chinese regional cuisines and a desire to share the flavors we came to love. Our menu is personal, reflecting memories of childhood, travel, and beloved family recipes.

Refined by our many years in fine dining and hospitality, Basilico is a collective return to the comforting dishes we love to make, eat, and share.
